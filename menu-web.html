Menu

Great food, always fresh

We have the perfect recipe to satisfy your appetite for food and fun. Our pizzas are made fresh, never frozen. We use only the freshest ingredients and toppings, and oven-bake each pizza to order. At our salad bar, we offer over 40 garden-fresh items to make a salad just the way you like it. Plus, we offer other great options like oven-baked sandwiches and mouthwatering buffalo wings.

Gluten-free the right way! Learn about our gluten-free pizza and dessert.

Pizza

Made Fresh to Order

Pizza dough made fresh in-house, never frozen, topped with our own tomato herb sauce, freshly shredded mozzarella cheese and your choice of great meat and vegetable toppings.

Toppings: Pepperoni, Bacon, Jalapeños, Chicken, Tomatoes, Mushrooms, Sausage, Beef, Pineapple, Black Olives, Red Onions, Canadian Bacon, Green Peppers

  • Medium (10 slices)
  • Large (12 slices)

Large French Fries

Chicken Nuggets
Oven-baked, all-white-meat chicken nuggets. Served with fresh fruit and your choice of oven-baked french fries, pasta salad or carrot slims, and lite ranch dressing.

Sampler Platter
Two orders of cheesy breadsticks, large order of oven-baked fries and your choice of any style wings.
