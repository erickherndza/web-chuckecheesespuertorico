Visita Chuck E. Cheese's

Your kids are going to grow up. 
Just not today, not if we can help it.

Chuck E. Cheese’s is the ultimate place Where A Kid Can Be A Kid®. And parents can enjoy every moment. That’s why Chuck E. himself takes pride in providing a safe, family-friendly environment that kids and parents will love coming back to. 

Admission is always free. So you’ll never overpay to play at Chuck E. Cheese’s.

Kid Check® Program

Before you get to all the food and fun, you will enter through our Kid Check® area.

Every member of a party – adult and kid; family or group – who enters Chuck E. Cheese’s gets a unique hand stamp that is verified upon their exit to assist in our objective that families who come together leave together.

This program is not a substitute for adult supervision.

HEALTHY HANDS
ARE CLEAN HANDS

For your convenience, Chuck E. Cheese's now offers complimentary Purell® hand sanitizer. Look for it near our entrance at Kid Check® and throughout the restaurant.

Prizes

After all the pizza has been eaten, games have been played, and fun has been had, take your ticket winnings over to our prize area to claim your kid's reward for a great day of fun. Your child gets the power to choose from the coolest Chuck E. merchandise to the hottest new toys and gizmos. Winning prizes can be the highlight of the whole trip, so make sure you choose wisely.

Games & Rides

From the coolest video games and high-tech simulator rides to our Skytubes® attraction and kiddie rides for the little ones – we have fun covered. To help separate the older kids from the younger kids, our game rooms are arranged into three unique sections:

  • Toddler Zone®
  • Kiddie Area
  • Skill Games & Arcades

And every zone can be a parents zone. Mom and Dad are free to relax and watch their kids explore and play along anywhere.

Attractions vary by location.

Entertainment

You’ve seen and heard Chuck E. rock out on TV. Now see it for yourself at our live stage show. 

Joining Chuck E. along the way will be his good friends and fellow band members Helen Henny, Jasper T. Jowls, Mr. Munch and Pasqually. 

After the show, Chuck E. will make the rounds to join the kids in-store, and maybe even teach them a move or two.
