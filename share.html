Make-A-Wish Partner

Chuck E. Cheese's is proud to partner with Make-A-Wish organizations to provide parties for seriously ill children. Every child deserves a place to go where they can go to just be a kid and it is especially important to us that those struggling with life-threatening illnesses remember to have fun. Daniel recently enjoyed a party before heading to Disney World to fulfill his wish of meeting Lightning McQueen!
