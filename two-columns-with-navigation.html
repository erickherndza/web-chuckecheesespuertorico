Birthdays

Throw a party to remember

Throw a magical party without lifting a finger! Our parties are a rocking way for your kids to celebrate with Chuck E. Cheese himself. All you do is relax, and we do all the work. What are you waiting for? Plan your party today.

Reserved Specials

Ticket Blaster®
The birthday child will be able to take a turn in the Ticket Blaster® for a chance to win the Magic Ticket worth 1,000 tickets.

Birthday child is guaranteed to win a minimum of 200 tickets. At participating locations.  

Weekday Specials
Avoid the crowd while having even more fun. Reserve a party Monday-Thursday and get unlimited time at your table.

Excludes school holidays.

Walk-In Parties

You are always welcome to celebrate all the fun and excitement Chuck E. Cheese's has to offer without reserving a party package.

You can even add a birthday crown, medallions, balloons and delicious cake to your walk-in party.

Unreserved parties do not include the birthday package items or a reserved table.
